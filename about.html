Our Story

What began as a small family business in 2021 has grown into a trusted cleaning service, serving hundreds of satisfied customers.

Founded by Tonny and Giuseppe Rossi, BlueWave Aquapro started with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services with unmatched personal service. We combine professional techniques with modern, eco-friendly technology.

We believe that every garment tells a story - from your favorite business suit to your wedding dress - and we're honored to help preserve those precious memories and moments.
