Our Story

What began as a small family business in 1998 has grown into the region's most trusted cleaning service, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Founded by Maria and Giuseppe Rossi, BlueWave Aquapro started with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services with unmatched personal service. Today, their children continue that tradition, combining time-tested techniques with modern, eco-friendly technology.

We believe that every garment tells a story - from your favorite business suit to your wedding dress - and we're honored to help preserve those precious memories and moments.
